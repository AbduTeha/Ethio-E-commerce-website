Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Return Policy

7-Day Return Window

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return for most products. To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition as received
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase
Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned:

  • Gift cards
  • Food items, coffee, spices, and perishable goods
  • Personal care products
  • Custom-made or personalized items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Downloadable software products
Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange for:

  • Free pickup of the damaged item
  • Replacement with the same item (if available)
  • Full refund if replacement is not available

Refund Policy

Refund Process

Once we receive your return, we will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved:

  1. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  2. The refund will be issued to your original payment method
  3. Bank transfers may take additional 2-3 business days
  4. You will receive an email confirmation when refund is processed
Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days:

  • Check your bank account again
  • Contact your credit card company (processing time may vary)
  • Contact your bank (bank transfers may take longer)
  • If still not resolved, contact us at refunds@ethioshop.com
Return Shipping Costs
  • Defective Items: Free return shipping
  • Change of Mind: Customer pays return shipping
  • Wrong Item Shipped: Free return shipping
