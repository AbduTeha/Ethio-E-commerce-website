Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping on EthioShop

To place an order:

  1. Browse products and click "Add to Cart"
  2. Go to your cart by clicking the cart icon
  3. Review your items and click "Proceed to Checkout"
  4. Enter your delivery information
  5. Confirm your order

You'll receive an order confirmation with your order ID.

We accept the following payment methods in Ethiopia:

  • Cash on Delivery: Pay when you receive your order
  • Bank Transfer: CBE, Dashen, Awash, Abyssinia Bank
  • Mobile Money: TeleBirr, M-Pesa, CBE Birr

Most customers prefer cash on delivery for convenience.

Delivery times vary by location:

  • Addis Ababa: 1-2 business days
  • Major Cities (Bahir Dar, Hawassa, etc.): 3-4 business days
  • Other Areas: 5-7 business days

You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

We offer a 7-day return policy for most products:

  • Products must be unused and in original packaging
  • Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Some items (food, perishables) cannot be returned

Contact our customer service to initiate a return.

Creating an account is easy:

  1. Click "Register" in the top navigation
  2. Fill in your personal information
  3. Choose your account type (Buyer or Seller)
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Start shopping or selling!

Accounts are free for buyers. Seller accounts require verification.
