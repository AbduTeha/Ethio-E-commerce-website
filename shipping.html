Delivery Information

Shipping Areas

We currently deliver to all major cities in Ethiopia:

  • Addis Ababa and surrounding areas
  • Bahir Dar, Gondar, and Amhara region
  • Hawassa, Awasa, and Sidama region
  • Mekelle, Tigray region
  • Dire Dawa, Harar, and eastern regions
  • Jimma, Oromia region
Delivery Times
Location Standard Delivery Express Delivery
Addis Ababa 1-2 business days Same day (order before 12 PM)
Major Cities 3-4 business days 2 business days
Other Areas 5-7 business days 3-4 business days
Shipping Costs
  • Free Shipping: Orders over ETB 1000 qualify for free standard shipping
  • Standard Shipping: ETB 50 for orders under ETB 1000
  • Express Shipping: ETB 100 additional charge
  • Rural Areas: Additional charges may apply based on location
Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive:

  • Tracking number via SMS and email
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Driver contact information on delivery day
  • Real-time updates via our mobile app
Delivery Process
  1. Order confirmation email sent immediately
  2. Order processed within 24 hours
  3. Shipping confirmation with tracking number
  4. Delivery attempt (driver will call before delivery)
  5. Payment collection (if cash on delivery)
  6. Order completion notification
Important Notes
  • Please ensure someone is available to receive the delivery
  • Have exact change ready for cash on delivery orders
  • Inspect your package before signing for delivery
  • Contact us immediately if there are any issues with delivery
